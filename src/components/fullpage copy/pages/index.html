About Us

The Bookware app will allow collectors to easily catalog their collection by finding and capturing product details for them. The app will also allow the user to interact with archived items reading summaries and reviews, adding personalized comments, ratings and rankings. The app will also support any transaction bookkeeping by capturing, purchase and sale activity, including market price, cost basis, and any sales prices. This app will allow collectors to easily take stock of their collection, regain appreciation for it, easily retrieve items from storage, and facilitate decision making for liquidations and additional acquisitions.
